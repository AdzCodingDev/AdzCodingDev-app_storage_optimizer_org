Private storage review

See what takes space. Keep control of what changes.

EchoSweep is designed to inspect files and media on your Android device, identify exact duplicates, review similar photos, and support deliberate cleanup without an EchoSweep account.

Read the Privacy Policy How device data is used

Product principles

Local-first by design

On-device analysis

File inventory, hashing, byte comparison, and similar-photo analysis are designed to run on the device.

No EchoSweep account

The app does not require registration, login, cloud sync, or an EchoSweep account for its storage workflows.

Explicit cleanup

Cleanup actions are user initiated. Available behavior may include quarantine, restoration, local trash, or permanent deletion depending on the app version and Android access.

Clear boundaries

What the app may inspect

EchoSweep may access file names, paths, sizes, dates, media types, file contents needed for duplicate comparison, and image data needed for similar-photo analysis, within the storage areas Android makes available and the permissions you grant.

No hidden website tracking

This website contains no analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, external fonts, or third-party JavaScript. It is hosted by GitHub Pages, whose platform may process standard web-request information under GitHub's own privacy terms.
